Air Battle (航空戦)

Determine air superiority before ground combat begins.

Key Stat
CAS (Combat Air Superiority)

Outcomes

  • ✈️ Air Superiority (優勢) — Enemy bombers deal less damage
  • ⚖️ Air Parity (同勢) — Standard bomber damage
  • 💥 Air Inferiority (劣勢) — Heavy bomber damage to your fleet
