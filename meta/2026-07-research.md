## 1. Basic Game Mechanics & Rules

### Overview
**Kantai Collection** (艦隊これくしょん, *Kantai Korekushon*), abbreviated as **KanColle** (艦これ, *KanKore*), is a Japanese free-to-play web browser game developed by Kadokawa Games and published by DMM.com. Launched April 23, 2013, the game features World War II warships personified as teenage girls and young adult women ("ship girls" / 艦娘, *kanmusu*).

### Core Gameplay Loop
- Player takes on the role of an **admiral** (提督, *teitoku*)
- Build squadrons composed of individual ship girl characters with different attributes
- Send squadrons out on missions and map sorties
- Combat is largely automated; manual actions include micromanagement like building/repairing ships
- Player can organize up to **four different fleets** simultaneously

### Progression Systems
1. **Experience & Leveling**: Kanmusu gain XP through battles, level up to Lv.99 (cap)
2. **Remodeling**: Ships unlock more advanced models at certain levels with stat improvements and visual changes
3. **Modernization** (近代化改修, *kindaika kaishu*): "Feed" unwanted kanmusu to other ships for attribute bonuses
4. **Map Advancement**: Progress through maps, gain XP through grinding
5. **Quest System**: Fulfill quests to obtain resources (fuel, ammo, steel, bauxite)
6. **Equipment Crafting**: New equipment can be crafted for ship girls

### Ship Girl Systems
- **316 unique ship girls** available (810 including remodels) as of September 2025
- Depicted by **32 illustrators**, voiced by **74 seiyūs**
- Physical characteristics correlate to historical ships:
  - Larger displacement ships (cruisers, battleships, carriers) → young women
  - Smaller ships (destroyers, coastal defense) → pubescent girls
- Foreign navies added over time: Soviet Navy, Kriegsmarine, Regia Marina, USN, Royal Navy, French Navy, Swedish Navy, Royal Netherlands Navy, Royal Australian Navy

### Equipment System
Ship girls can be customized with various equipment slots adding attribute bonuses and special effects:
- **Naval guns**, anti-aircraft guns, torpedoes
- **Aircraft**: torpedo bombers, dive bombers, fighters, seaplanes, recon planes
- **Support gear**: radars, steam turbines, special artillery shells, depth charges, sonars, drum canisters, searchlights, anti-torpedo bulges

### Resource Management
- Four core resources: **Fuel**, **Ammo**, **Steel**, **Bauxite**
- Ships accumulate fatigue after sorties (decreased attributes)
- Sparked ships (critical damage) have severely reduced combat effectiveness
- Free-to-play with optional microtransactions for premium items

### Combat & PvP
- Exercise maneuvers menu for player-vs-player battles on same server
- In-game ranking boards with periodical prizes for top players
- **20 servers** as of January 2016, each named after a WWII-era Japanese naval base

## 4. Development History & Game Versions

### Flash Era (1st Sequence)
- Original game on Adobe Flash engine
- Discontinued August 15, 2018 with Flash EOL

### HTML5 Port (2nd Sequence)
- Full port to HTML5 engine with higher resolution and frame rate
- Extended October 17, 2025 with further improvements

### Spin-off Games
- **KanColle Kai** (艦これ改): PS Vita turn-based strategy game, released Feb 18, 2016. Features hexagonal world maps, resource transfer system with escort missions. Famitsu score: 29/40, sold 140,757 physical copies first week.
- **KanColle Arcade** (艦これアーケード): Sega AM2 trading card arcade game launched April 26, 2016. Features fully modeled 3D characters with directional control in combat.

## 5. Community & Media

### Player Base
- Launched April 23, 2013; reached 3 million registered players by April 2015
- Peak: 800,000+ regular active players (Sept 2013), 600,000 in one week spike (Aug 2013)
- Over 345,000 different artworks on Pixiv tagged "KanColle" by April 2015

### Media Franchise
- Multiple manga series (4koma format, story-driven adaptations)
- Light novels including *Bonds of the Wings of Cranes*
- Official tabletop RPG (released March 18, 2014)
- Anime TV series (2015), anime film (2016)
- Vocal music collections and soundtracks
